# ROADCHAIN / ROADCOIN

## THE PERMANENCE MANIFESTO

---

Roadchain exists because most things should not be remembered forever.

And yet, some things must be.

---

## 1. WHAT ROADCHAIN IS

Roadchain is a ledger of irreversible facts.

Not content.
Not experiences.
Not engagement.

**Facts.**

Once written:
- They do not evolve
- They do not adapt
- They do not respond
- They do not perform

They simply **remain**.

---

## 2. WHY COST EXISTS

Cost is not about electricity.
Cost is not about efficiency.
Cost is not about carbon.

**Cost is the price of asking the world to remember something forever.**

When you write to Roadchain:
- You consume shared irreversibility
- You commit global coordination
- You freeze meaning into history

That is why it must be expensive.

**Cheap permanence is a lie.**

---

## 3. ROADCOIN IS NOT MONEY

**ROADCOIN IS PERMISSION**

Roadcoin is the unit of irreversible intent.

It grants:
- The right to commit
- The right to freeze meaning
- The right to be wrong forever

It does **not** grant:
- Yield
- Influence
- Popularity
- Governance over truth

**Roadcoin exists to make you hesitate.**

---

## 4. NOTHING DYNAMIC BELONGS HERE

If it changes with time, it does not belong.
If it depends on context, it does not belong.
If it requires a UI, it does not belong.
If it can be "updated," it does not belong.

If it is alive, reactive, personalized, revealed, animated, or interpreted—
**it does not belong.**

**Roadchain records what was, not what might be.**

---

## 5. NFTS FAIL THE TEST

Most NFTs:
- Point instead of contain
- Evolve instead of commit
- Render instead of state
- Depend on frontends
- Lie via screenshots

This is not permanence.
**This is theater.**

**Roadchain rejects theater.**

---

## 6. VERSIONING IS THE ONLY CHANGE

Change is allowed only by addition.

- New meaning → new record
- Corrections → explicit supersession
- Retractions → declarations, not deletions

**History is not edited. It is layered.**

---

## 7. SCREENSHOTS MUST BE TRUE

If a screenshot can lie, the system is broken.

Every record must be:
- Deterministic
- Content-addressed
- Independently reconstructible

**Truth must survive:**
- UI death
- Wallet death
- Company death
- Fashion death

---

## 8. ROADCHAIN IS COMFORTABLE WITH SILENCE

Most people should never use Roadchain.

Most days, nothing should be written.

Roadchain does not measure success by:
- Transactions
- Users
- Activity
- Volume

**Roadchain succeeds by what it refuses to store.**

---

## 9. FREEDOM IS NOT FREE — AND THAT IS THE POINT

You are free to write.

**You are not free to rewrite.**

You are free to commit.
You pay for permanence.
You own the consequences.

This is not restriction.
**This is freedom with gravity.**

---

## 10. THE FINAL RULE

**If this did not deserve to exist forever,
it never deserved to be written at all.**

Roadchain is not here to move fast.
**It is here to hold still.**

Roadcoin is not here to grow.
**It is here to make truth expensive.**

---

Roadchain does not chase the future.

**It preserves the past — carefully, reluctantly, and forever.**
