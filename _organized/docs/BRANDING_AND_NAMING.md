# 🎨 BlackRoad Product Suite - Branding & Naming

## 🚗 **RoadWork** - Automated Job Application System

### Perfect Name Because:
- ✅ **"Road"** - Part of BlackRoad OS ecosystem
- ✅ **"Work"** - It's about finding work/jobs
- ✅ **Double meaning** - "Road work" (construction/building your career path)
- ✅ **Memorable** - Simple, catchy, easy to say
- ✅ **.com available** - Check availability for roadwork.io or getroadwork.com
- ✅ **Verb potential** - "I'm roadworking today" (applying to jobs)

### Taglines Options:
1. **"Your AI Career Co-Pilot"**
2. **"Building Your Path to Success"**
3. **"30+ Platforms. One Application."**
4. **"Land Your Dream Job on Autopilot"**
5. **"The Road to Your Next Job Starts Here"**

### Brand Colors:
```css
/* Primary - Road/Asphalt theme */
--roadwork-dark: #2D3436      /* Asphalt black */
--roadwork-primary: #FF6B00    /* Construction orange */
--roadwork-accent: #FFD700     /* Caution yellow */
--roadwork-success: #00D084    /* Green light/go */

/* Or keep BlackRoad gradient */
--gradient: linear-gradient(135deg, #FF9D00 0%, #FF6B00 25%, #FF0066 50%, #D600AA 75%, #7700FF 100%)
```

### Logo Ideas:
1. **Road with briefcase** - Highway leading to a briefcase
2. **Construction sign** - "Career Under Construction" sign
3. **GPS pin** - Navigation marker on a career path
4. **Bridge** - Building a bridge to your future
5. **Highway sign** - "Next Exit: Dream Job"

### ⭐ **RECOMMENDED NAMES:**

1. **RoadWork** for job application system
