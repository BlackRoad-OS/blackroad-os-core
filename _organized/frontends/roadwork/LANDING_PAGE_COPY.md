## 🧪 MESSAGING PRINCIPLES

### Tone
- **Direct** - No marketing fluff
- **Technical** - Respect the user's intelligence
- **Honest** - No false promises
- **Professional** - Serious tool for serious people

### Voice
- **Active** - "Apply smarter" not "Applications are applied"
- **Confident** - "ROADWORK™ will refuse" not "ROADWORK™ might refuse"
- **Clear** - "No lies policy" not "Truth-oriented approach"

- ❌ "Revolutionary"

- ❌ "Disruptive"
- ❌ "10x your career"
- ❌ "Hack the system"

- ✅ "Secure"
- ✅ "Honest"
- ✅ "Professional"
- ✅ "Tracked"
