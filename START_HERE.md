## What Is BlackRoad OS?

It's an AI-powered operating system that runs in your browser.

- **Agents** do tasks for you automatically
- **RoadWork** finds and tracks job applications
- **Prism** shows you what's happening in real time
- **Truth Engine** double-checks information so you don't have to

You don't need to know what any of that means to use it. Just open the app.
